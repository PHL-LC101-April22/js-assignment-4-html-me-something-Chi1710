Beef Chow Fun Recipes

Main ingredients

  • 10 oz flank steak
  • 1 lb fresh flat rice noodles
  • 3 stems green onions
  • 0.50 yellow onion
  • 1 handful bean sprouts
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Beef Marinade

Beef Marinade

  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp Kikkoman® Oyster Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Kikkoman® Soy Sauce
  • 1 tsp Kikkoman® Tamari (Kikkoman offers regular Tamari and Gluten Free Tamari, a wheat-free version.)
  • 0.25 tsp white pepper
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp cooking wine
  • 1 tsp sugar

Chow Fun Sauce

  • 1 tsp Kikkoman® Tamari (Kikkoman offers regular Tamari and Gluten Free Tamari, a wheat-free version.)
  • 1 tbsp Kikkoman® Soy Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Kikkoman® Oyster Sauce

Ingredients for Cooking

  • 1 tsp Kikkoman® Sesame Oil
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil

Steps

  1. Cut & wash beef

    2. Cut your flank steak (10 oz) into medium or 1/4 inch slices. Once cut, put the meat into a room-temperature water bath to rinse out the myoglobin.

  2. Prepare vegetables

    3. Peel and thinly slice the yellow onion (0.50 ) into 1/4 inch thickness. Wash the bean sprouts (1 handful), drain, and set aside. Wash and chop the green onions (3 stems) into 1 inch long pieces. Use all of the green onions and separate the white parts from the green parts.

  3. Create Beef marinade

    4. In a medium-sized bowl, mix the baking soda (1 tsp), cornstarch (1 tbsp), oyster sauce (1 tbsp), soy sauce (1 tbsp), tamari (1 tsp), white pepper (0.25 tsp), water (2 tbsp), cooking wine (1 tsp), sugar (1 tsp), and mix until everything is incorporated and smooth.

  4. Mix beef into marinade

    5. Drain the meat from the water bath, squeeze out the excess water. Add the meat into the marinade and mix until the meat is fully coated in the marinade and set it aside.

  5. Prepare rice noodles

    6. Prepare the rice noodles (1 lb) by separating the noodles if you see them stuck together.

  6. Create chow fun sauce

    7. In a small bowl, mix tamari (1 tsp), soy sauce (1 tbsp), and oyster sauce. Once mixed, set it aside.

  7. stir-fry beef

    8. Add cooking oil for about 1 min then add your beef and cook for about 1 min on high heat. Remove the beef and set it aside.

  8. Stir-Fry vegetables & noodles

    9. Fry the yellow onion and white part of the scallions 1st for about a min, then add the rice noodles and cook for 2 mins. Add the sauce, green parts of the scallion and bean sprouts for about a min

  9. Add beef back

    10. Add the beef and mix. Turn off the heat and add the sesame oil

Steamed Egg Recipes

Main Ingredients

  • 4 egg
  • 2 cup warm water
  • 2 green onion
  • 0.50 tsp salt
  • 0.50 tsp chicken bouillon
  • 0.50 tbsp oil
  • white pepper(to taste)

Steps

  1. Cut green onion

    2. Chop the green onions to be added as garnish at the end

  2. Boil water in the wok

    3. Place the steamer rack in the wok and place your bowl on top of the rack. Pour enough water so that the bowl is about half way submerged in the wok. Take the bowl out, and place lid on the wok. Let the warer come to boil.

  3. Start mixing eggs

    4. Crack the eggs (4 ) into a bowl, and add salt (0.50 tsp), chicken bouillon (0.50 tsp), and oil (0.50 tbsp). Start mixing the eggs with a fork or chopstick.

  4. Prepare warm water and mix with eggs

    5. Pour a quarter of the warm water (0.50 cup) into the eggs, and mix until bubbles start foaming at the top. Pour the remaining warm water (1.5 cup), and continue mixing until you see bubbles again. The ideal range of the water temp is 35-45 C or 95-113 F.

  5. Start steaming eggs

    6. The water in the wok should be boiling now. Lower the heat and remove the lid. Carefully place your bowl inside with an oevn mitt. Pour your egg and water mixture into the bowl and cover the wok.

  6. Boil simmers, wait

    7. After you cover the wok, leave the stove on high heat until water starts boiling again. Once is boiling, lower the heat to a simmer and cook for about 8 mins.

  7. Add garnish and flavor

    8. Once the dish is done steaming, remove the lid. Add green onion and white pepper and soy sauce.

  8. Remove bowl from wok

    9. Depending on how much clearance you have, put on oven mitts and carefully life the bowl out of the wok.

Sweet Corn Pork Rib Soup Recipes

Main Ingredients

  • 2 Sweet Corn
  • 1 Kg Pork Spare Ribs
  • 5-6 Chinese Dried Shiitake Mushrooms
  • 6 red dates
  • 1 carrot large
  • 1 TBsp wolfberries
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 3.5 liters water

Steps

  1. Wash the spare ribs, then blanche it with the boiling water, then wash and drain.
  2. Wash the mushrooms, then soak in hot water for 15 minutes. Don't discard the mushroom water.
  3. Wash, peel and chop the carrot and onion into small chunks. Cut the sweet corn into 3 or 4 pieces.
  4. In a large pot, bring water to boil. Then add in pork ribs, mushroom and the mushroom water and boil for half an hour.
  5. Next, add in the sweet corn, carrots, onions and continue to boil for another 1.5 hours at medium heat.
  6. Lastly, add in the red dates and wolf berries, then boil for another 15 minutes. Add salt to taste, and soup is ready to be served!
